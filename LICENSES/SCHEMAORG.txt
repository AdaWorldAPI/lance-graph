Usage in this repository
------------------------
data/ontologies/schemaorg.ttl is the canonical schema.org ontology
(current HTTPS variant, ~1400 named classes and properties), hydrated
by lance-graph-ontology as the L3 commercial-web slot
OGIT::SCHEMAORG_V1.

Declares inherits_from: Some(OGIT::DOLCE_V1.0). Provides the
Thing / Person / Organization / Place / Event / Product / CreativeWork
hierarchy, plus schema.org's distinctive flexible-typing surface
(domainIncludes / rangeIncludes) that the cognitive-shader cascade
treats as soft domain/range constraints.
