Signature note (divergence from the brief's illustrative `[u32; K]`): value(code) reads
the palette centroid (`Base17.dims`, signed i16), so the accumulator must be signed;
u32 cannot hold a signed reduce. K = BASE_DIM = 17 (Base17 dim count). Accumulates in
i64 (weight u16 up to 65535 × value i16 up to 32767, summed over up to 256 contribs —
comfortably inside i64, no overflow risk). Reduce = Sum_i weight_i * value(code_i) per
Base17 dimension, exactly as specified.
